** systemd-oomd                                                       :manual:
Userspace OOM killer that acts on PSI/swap pressure before the kernel
deadlocks. Companion to the user-side config in [[file:background.org::*Out-of-memory handling][background.org]].

Setup:
- =sudo systemctl enable --now systemd-oomd.service=
- =sudo cp ~/.haris/provision/files/99-haris.conf /etc/systemd/oomd.conf.d/99-haris.conf=
- =sudo cp ~/.haris/provision/files/50-oomd.conf /etc/systemd/system/user@.service.d/50-oomd.conf=
- ~sudo systemctl daemon-reload~

*** /etc/systemd/oomd.conf.d/99-haris.conf
[[file:provision/files/99-haris.conf]]

*** /etc/systemd/system/user@.service.d/50-oomd.conf
Required so oomd has authority to kill cgroups inside the user manager
(notably =app.slice=, which the user-side drop-in opts in).

** zram                                                               :manual:
8 GiB zram device backed by zstd compression, placed in front of the 64 GiB
swapfile. The kernel fills zram first (~50× lower latency than NVMe), spilling
to disk only under sustained pressure.

Setup:
- ~sudo pacman -S zram-generator~
- =sudo cp ~/.haris/provision/files/zram-generator.conf /etc/systemd/zram-generator.conf=
- =sudo cp ~/.haris/provision/files/99-zram.conf /etc/sysctl.d/99-zram.conf=
- ~sudo sysctl -p /etc/sysctl.d/99-zram.conf~
- ~sudo systemctl daemon-reload~
- ~sudo systemctl start systemd-zram-setup@zram0~

*** /etc/systemd/zram-generator.conf
[[file:provision/files/zram-generator.conf]]

*** /etc/sysctl.d/99-zram.conf
Overrides the =vm.swappiness = 10= in =99-haris-vm.conf= (sorts after it
lexicographically). With fast zram swap, swappiness >100 tells the kernel to
prefer reclaiming anonymous pages to zram over evicting page cache.
[[file:provision/files/99-zram.conf]]

** sysctl: reduce swappiness                                          :manual:
With 27 GiB RAM and an NVMe-backed 31 GiB swap, the default swappiness of 60
is far too eager — by the time the system feels slow, ~14 GiB is already
swapped out and reclaim thrashes. Lower it so dirty pages stay in RAM longer.
[[file:provision/files/99-haris-vm.conf]]
